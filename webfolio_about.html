Introduction

Xin Chao! My name is Vinh, a third-year student in Applied Computing: Interactive System Design

How did I find my passion?

I first encountered Pokemon when I was three years old and have been with them ever since. Aside from Pokemon and many other great franchise series, my own curiosity about the digital side of both design and coding has piqued my interest. Throughout my grade school years, I continually engage with these creative opportunities, constantly asking myself what career would best suit my interests. At the age of 18, I got into University of Science - major in Electronics and Telecommunication in Vietnam; nonetheless, I knew it was not what I am looking for my career path. In the winter of 2020, I moved and started studying as a full-time student at the University of Saskatchewan. However, I wasn't sure about my major and didn't ask to declare it. It wasn't until my sophomore year that I finally knew which career path suited me best (e.g. front-end developing, digital designing and an efficient amount of back-end knowledge). Now that I have a clearer direction, I am learning and working on myself to be more involved with my field.

More about Me?

I am a friend and I advise, and get advised of a small number of high school/freshmen and IT-experienced ones both academically and professionally. As my previous work and associations have helped me make many connections with others, I have had many academic opportunities to advise and discuss with others. As I stay connecting with other people, it helps me develop new ideas, get advice on career paths, and offer guidance in times of uncertainty. In general, this is one of the best ways to learn new skills and grow both professionally and academically.

I am actively joining digital design/programming/game groups! I find that these groups offer a great way to connect with others and provide many knowledge both professionally and practically. These groups also help me understand the community around me and develop a more inclusive and growth mindset.
